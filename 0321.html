Install Kodi on Amazon FireTV Devices

  1. Install the Downloader application
  2. Launch Downloader
  3. Navigate to https://kodi.tv
  4. Scroll down and click on the Android logo
  5. Scroll down again and click on the Android logo
  6. Scroll down and click on the ARMV7A (32 bit) link
  7. A prompt will display to go to settings and allow installing unknown apps
  8. Click Settings > Install Unknown Apps > Downloader > Toggle the setting to On
  9. Click back a few times to return to the Downloader app
  10. Click Install and then Install again
